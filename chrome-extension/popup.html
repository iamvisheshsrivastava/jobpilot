How to use JobPilot

1
Log in with your JobPilot account — done!
2
Navigate to any job posting (LinkedIn, Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, or any site)
3
Click ⊕ Capture — AI extracts title, company & deadline automatically
4
Optionally click ✦ Check Fit for an AI suitability verdict
5
Choose category and click Save Job to Dashboard

API Keys & Settings

Add your Groq or OpenRouter API key in the Settings page of the dashboard to power AI features.
