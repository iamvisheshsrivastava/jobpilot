How to use JobPilot

1
Log in with your JobPilot account (already done!)
2
Navigate to any job posting on any website
3
Click Capture This Job — AI will extract the title, company, and deadline
4
Optionally click Check Fit to see an AI suitability verdict
5
Choose your category and click Save Job

Settings & API Keys

Go to the Settings page in the dashboard to add your Groq or OpenRouter API key. These power the AI features.
